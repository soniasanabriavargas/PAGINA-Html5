TO PARTICIPATE IN THIS PROGRAM STUDENTS MUST:

  • Have a grade point average of 8.5 or higher, and be accepted into a university or vocational school program.

  • Demonstrate financial need to pursue their studies.

  • Have selected a major which will afford them gainful employment after graduation.

  • have family support for their goals.

STEPS FOR SUCCESS

  • Application: An extensive application is required from all potential students.

  • Interview & Vetting: A visit is arranged in the student´s home and submitted information is verified.

  • Monitoring: Students meet with UVA at the close of each semester to present grades and discuss issues.

  • Consistent: All rules are applied equally to all applicants without exception.

  • Fund distribution: 100% of donations for students go to the students. UVA has no administrative cost.

  • Transparency: UVA financial status is reviewable by donors at any time.

  • Professional Licensing: UVA assists in covering necessary expenses.

  • Donor Loyalty: is a testament to the confidence in the program over the years.
